conveyancing is the transfer of legal title of real property from one person to another, or the granting of an encumbrance such as a mortgage or alien, it may also be defined as the art or science of effecting the transfer of legal property or modifying interest in relation to property by means of a (written) document.  As such the three critical ingredients are thus the process, the legal title and the transfer or modification.
